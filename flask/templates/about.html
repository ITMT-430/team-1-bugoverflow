Bug Overflow

This project was created and collaborated between Zubin Fanibanda, Alfredo Fernandez, Neil Okhandiar and Brandon Sansone.

The purpose of this project is to create a gallery of unidentied bugs where users can come and share their knowledge about that bug.

Based on the designs and functionality of Stack Overflow.
